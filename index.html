Certifications CompTIA

I obtained the Computing Technology Industry Association (CompTIA)'s A+, Network+, and Security+ certifications in July 2018 to validate entry-level security knowledge I obtained during my freshman year at RIT through classes, clubs, and security competitions.

I plan to obtain the Cisco Certified Networking Associate (CCNA) certification in Routing & Switching or the CompTIA PenTest+ certification during RIT's 2018-19 winter intercession. It is my goal to eventually prepare myself to pursue certifications such as OSCP and CISSP later during my studies at RIT.


Awards ACT, NFIB

I achieved Exemplar status during the American College Test's 2017 College and Career Readiness Campaign due to exam performance and my efforts to bring science, technology, engineering, and math educational opportunities to my high school in rural northern Minnesota.

I was also awarded the National Federation of Independent Business's Young Entrepreneur Award for Minnesota for founding Tauber IT Solutions to offer IT configuration and electronics repair services to small businesses in my community.


Activism
